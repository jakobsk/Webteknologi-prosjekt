P1 Project Requirements

Audun, Jakob, Per, Torgeir & Øystein

17.september 2019

Our client

Our client is two sports interested friends that has decided to create their own online webshop for selling sports equipment. Their names are Odin and Are Odin and they are both 30 years old.

Purpose and goals

This website is the core component of the business model of the firm, as it is an online Sport shop without any physical stores. Its purpose is thus to display all the items that XXXL currently has in its storage facility. The overall aim of the website is to make revenue for the company. Lastly, the website also serves as a recruitment platform for new employees. XXXL wishes to compete with already established competitors and therefore needs an increasing workforce.

Audience

The website will attract people who are interested in sports, travelling and physical activities. Since XXXL aims to have a broad selection of items to offer its customers, the audience will range from the professional athlete to the regular man on the streets who simply likes to exercise.

Content of the website and organization

  1. "Front page" The front page is the landing page of the website. It will contain selected offers and clickable pictures of the different categories that will send the user to the category page.
  2. "About" This section will describe the business concept of XXXL and who the persons behind the concept are. It will have a picture of the creators of the webstore and some text about their background. It will also describe why XXXL is the best alternative when shopping sports equipment online.
  3. "Categories"
    1. "Football"
    2. "Running"
    3. "Tennis"
    This section will give you three alternatives when clicking on it. When clicking one of the three alternatives the user will go to a page that shows pictures of available sports equipment in each category. It will have the price and a brief decscription of each item. When clicking an item the user will go to a new page with more information about the item. Each item will have an option “add to cart”, so the user can continue shopping.
  4. "Contact us" This page will contain an e-mail for customer support. It will also have a e-mail for people that want’s to apply for a job.
  5. "Shopping Cart" This will show all the items the user has added to the shopping cart in that session.

Functional and non-functional requirements

  • The owners want’s the website to have a clean and simple user interface
  • The owners have specifically told us that they would like to be able to modify the content of the website. Thus, we plan to use Javascript to write the content.
