Purpose and goals

This website is the core component of the business model of the firm, as it is an online Sport shop without any physical stores. Its purpose is thus to display all the items that XXXL currently has in its storage facility. The overall aim of the website is to make revenue for the company. Lastly, the website also serves as a recruitment platform for new employees. XXXL wishes to compete with already established competitors and therefore needs an increasing workforce.
